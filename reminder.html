GENTLE REMINDER: COMMUNAL GRASS AREA - VANDALISM/ABUSE/NUISANCE/ANTISOCIAL BEHAVIOUR

Due to vandalism, abuse, Nuisance and Antisocial Behaviour (ASB), residents will be given access to the communal grass area between Addington and Thornicroft houses by the housing office. Residents must comply with the following conditions before access is granted. The office will not allow access to any resident who breaches the conditions. Conditions for use are:
  • No Nuisance or ASB
  • No loud music
  • No screaming or shouting
  • The place must be kept clean after use
  • No smoking or drinking alcohol
  • No dog fouling
  • No abuse or foul language
  • No ball games
  • All events must end at 9 pm
If residents would like to use the green area for a quiet picnic, please contact the office to complete a form and collect the fob key. You are required to return the fob key the next working day after use. The decision to restrict access to the green space has been necessitated due to the misuse by some residents.
